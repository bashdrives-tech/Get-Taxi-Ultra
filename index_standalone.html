Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have compiled precise answers on state permit taxes, toll logs, and night halt charges.

Your estimate includes the fuel charges, professional driver allowance (driver beta), and state border permit taxes if climbing across state borders (e.g., to Munnar or Wayanad). High road toll-gate fees, airport entry logs, and hill parking logs are extra as actual bill receipts are generated.
For multi-day packages, the driver allowance (driver beta) is already added into your final invoice estimate. It covers the driver’s basic food and lodging requirements. You do not need to provide hotel rooms or meals for our chauffeurs.
Outstation multi-day tours are subject to a minimum average distance of 250 KM per day. If you drive less, you will be billed for 250 KM per day. If your total travel distance exceeds the total included KM (minimum 250 KM x number of days), overage is billed at standard rates (₹15/KM for Sedan, ₹18/KM for SUV Prime, ₹22/KM for Premium SUV).
